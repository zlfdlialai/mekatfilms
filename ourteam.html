Our Team

Meet the team behind Mekat Films.

Hari Ahmad

Position: Co-Founder

Hari is a Kiput filmmaker based in Miri, Sarawak. He has directed and written films for Mekat Films since its early days. The self taught director has been nominated for The Next New Wave filmmaker of Seashort Film Festival and Mini Film Festival.

Adi Zechariah

Position: Co-Founder

Adi is a Melanau cinematographer based in Miri, Sarawak. Who has experienced being the director of photography for multiple short films, documentaries and feature films.

Mohamad Zulfadli

Position: Junior editor / Intern

Zulfadli is an intern at Mekat Films and is currently pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science in UNIMAS. Despite his acedemic focus, he has chosen to explore film production and at the same web developing through his internship. At 23 years old, he is gaining valuable experience in these fields.
